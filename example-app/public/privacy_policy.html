Privacy Notice

This version of our Privacy Notice became effective on 20 February 2020.

From time to time we will need to update this notice. Where changes are significant we’ll let all our customers know by sending an email to all Account Owners.

Who are we?

We are UneeQ: We provide a technology platform which is the global standard for digital humans- enabling the best creative minds to design and build amazing experiences that improve human life.

We, our or us: When we say we, us, or our we mean UneeQ Limited and its wholly owned subsidiaries. Our headquarters are in New Zealand but we have teams across the US, Australia and Europe too, made up of incredible and experienced AI practitioners, scientists, inventors, developers, quality experts, project managers, marketers and dreamers.

More information: If you want to learn more about us then you can visit our website and see how you can contact us, or where our nearest office to you is.

Our privacy principles

Do the right thing: We want to do the right thing when it comes to privacy. This means we not only adhere to privacy law but we do what people would expect of us.

Transparency: We want to be clear with our customers and with End Users (people who interact with digital humans) how our Platform processes Personal Data.

Secure: We want our system to be the best it can be and part of that is securely processing the information that we do, including any Personal Data.

Types of Personal Data Processed

There are 2 broad categories of personal data that we process:

User Data: which is information collected and generated about End Users’ interactions with a digital human using our Platform.

Customer Data: which is information we collect about current and prospective Customers through our website, by our sales team, or provided to us through the process of signing up to use our Platform.

User Data

There are a number of ways a customer can configure our Platform that changes the type of User Data that we gain access to and use. We require each of our customers to consider their particular use of our Services and share with End Users correct information about the User Data that is being collected and gain the consents that are required.

The User Data we collect and use during any given interaction with a digital human may be less than the description of the User Data we collect in this Privacy Notice, depending on how a customer has configured our Platform.

User Data we may collect

Audio from an End User: We may collect audio of what an End User is saying to a digital human.

Transcripts of the conversation: We may collect text transcripts of what an End User has said to a digital human, and what the digital human said in reply.

Video data of an End User: We may collect video of an End User interacting with a digital human.

How we use User Data

Our interest in using your User Data is because we are required to by contract to provide you our Services or because we have your consent to use it. Specifically we use it to:

Enable a digital human to understand you: We use audio data so that a digital human can understand what you are saying to it. In order to do this we share audio data with a third party service partners who helps us with transcription. Neither they or we store any audio User Data once we have completed the transcription.

Enable the digital human to behave appropriately: We use video data to allow a digital human to behave more intelligently. For example, to know when someone is speaking to it more accurately. We do not keep any video User Data at the end of an interaction.

Troubleshoot problems with the Platform: We may inspect conversation transcripts if we are investigating a problem with our Platform that we have been made aware of..

Aside from third party service partners who help us utilise the User Data in this way we do not share or sell User Data to anyone unless we have consent, a legal requirement to do so, or are acting on lawful instructions from a customer.

Customer Data

Customer Data we collect

Information you provide to us: You may provide us Customer Data such as your name, email address, phone number when signing up for an account to use our Platform, exchanging information with our team at an event, or filling out a form on our website to receive more information or to contact us.

Information an Account Owner provides to us: You may be granted access to our Platform by an account owner or someone else who is authorised. During this process they may provide us Customer Data including your email address.

Information we collect automatically: Our website digitalhumans.com collects some information automatically when you visit. This includes what pages you looked at and links you clicked on. Some of this information is collected using cookies or other similar tracking approaches.

How we use Customer Data

Our interest in using your Customer Data is because we are required to by contract to provide you our Services or because we have your consent to use it. Specifically we use it to:

Providing you access to our Platform: We use it to enable you to register, login and access our Platform and to support you if you have technical questions about our Platform.

Communicating with you: We also use it to communicate to you important information about our Platform, to provide you information you have requested, and also send marketing communications. You are always able to control what types of communications you receive from us.

Improving our digitalhuman.com website: We use it to understand how people are using our website digitalhumans.com and how we can improve it to provide a better experience for visitors.

For marketing purposes: We use Customer Data to display targeted advertising to you online on our own websites or through third party websites and their platforms.

Where we process data

Platform locations: We maintain Platform instances in Australia, Singapore, USA and the European Union. Your personal data may be processed in one of those locations.

Data transfers: When we share data to a service provider, or, when data is accessed by our authorized team members as part of ensuring our Platform is operating correctly, data can be transferred to another country. In all cases these destination countries are ones identified as providing adequate protection for European Economic Area (EEA) data. For example, New Zealand.

How we keep it secure

Our approach: We employ a number of technical measures and processes to protect your personal data. These security measures are different depending on the type of data and what we are using it for but you can read more details about these security measures.

Service providers: We also take steps to ensure our service providers also have appropriate security measures in place to protect any personal data they are processing on our behalf.

Your rights

You are always welcome to contact us at privacy@uneeq.com if you have questions about our Privacy Notice or your Personal Data. You can ask us:

What data we may hold about you.

To send a copy of any data we hold about you.

To remove data we may hold about you.

To withdraw your consent to us processing your data in the future.
